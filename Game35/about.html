About

What is Typing Master?

Well, as you can guess by the name, it is a typing game where you can increase your typing speed and typing accuracy by playing it daily. It's a funniest way to increase your typing speed, as well as your ability to read the words correctly just at a glance. Now you don't need to look for extra free time to practice to increase your typing ability, you can sit anywhere and start playing this game. It's a single page like game so you can play it on any device. Just open the game, start playing and increase your typing speed day by day

How to play?

Playing it is quite simple, you just need to type the word that appears on your screen in the section given just below the word. There will only be a time of a few seconds for each word, the shortest the word the shorter the time period. Type the word correctly as fast as you can, and once you type it correctly the next word will automatically appear, keep typing and new words will automatically keep appearing. If you mistype any word you will lose, then type the appeared word again and your score will restart. Same thing will happen if you can't type the given word in the given time. Keep playing as long as you can and increase your typing speed.

Who can play?

Anyone who wants to increase their typing speed can play this game. Of course, students are the ones who will play it the most, but a teacher, a parent, or any other person who wants to increase their typing speed can play it. This game has been designed to enhance typing ability in a fun way. Also those who want to increase their focus, can also use it for that purpose.

Why should you play it?

The most simple reason to play this game is to increase your typing ability. It is a single page and very light web game so unlike some other typing games you don't need a decent device to play it, you can play it on any smartphone of any specifications. The purpose of creating this game is to improve the typing speed of the player while learning new words at the same time. So by playing it you will not only increase your typing speed but also keep your vocabulary good.

Benefits of playing it?

Its name is Typing Master, so it will definitely make you a master of typing but this is not the only benefit of playing it. Since you have to type the words very fast after seeing it, it will automatically increase your focus. Good typing speed and accuracy are very important skills to have nowadays. Whether you are learning to type or want to improve your skills, you can do it with this typing game. The more you play this game the more your typing speed and accuracy get enhanced. You can reach a decent typing speed and accuracy by playing it for a couple of minutes on a daily basis.
